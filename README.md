# Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

Blinkit is one of India's leading quick-commerce platforms that delivers groceries and daily essentials to customers. This project focuses on analyzing Blinkit's sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution to uncover key business insights and support data-driven decision-making.

The analysis is performed using Excel and Power BI, and the findings are presented through an interactive dashboard.

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance across different product categories
* Evaluate customer satisfaction through ratings
* Understand inventory distribution and product demand
* Identify factors driving sales performance
* Build an interactive dashboard for business monitoring and decision-making

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

* Microsoft Excel → Data cleaning and preprocessing
* Power BI → Data modeling, visualization, and dashboard development
* Power Query → Data transformation and preparation
* DAX → KPI calculations and business metrics

## 📊 Dataset Information

The dataset contains information related to:

* Product categories
* Item types
* Outlet establishment year
* Outlet size
* Outlet location type
* Outlet type
* Sales amount
* Customer ratings
* Item visibility
* Fat content classification

## 📈 Key Business Insights

### 🔹 1. Total Business Performance

The business generated over $1.20M in total sales with an average sale value of approximately $141 per transaction.

👉 Insight: Blinkit maintains strong revenue generation across multiple product categories.

### 🔹 2. Fruits & Vegetables Lead Sales

Fruits and Vegetables contributed the highest sales among all product categories.

👉 Insight: Fresh products remain the primary revenue driver and should receive inventory priority.

### 🔹 3. Medium-Sized Outlets Perform Best

Medium-sized outlets generated the highest share of total sales compared to small and large outlets.

👉 Insight: Medium-sized stores provide the optimal balance between operational efficiency and revenue generation.

### 🔹 4. Tier 3 Locations Generate Strong Revenue

Tier 3 locations contributed significantly to overall sales performance.

👉 Insight: Demand for quick-commerce services is rapidly growing beyond major metropolitan areas.

### 🔹 5. Customer Satisfaction Remains High

The average customer rating remained above 3.9 out of 5 across outlets.

👉 Insight: Customers generally have a positive shopping experience with Blinkit.

### 🔹 6. Supermarket Type 1 Drives Maximum Sales

Among all outlet types, Supermarket Type 1 contributed the largest portion of revenue.

👉 Insight: This outlet format is the most effective sales channel and can be prioritized for future expansion.

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Increase inventory allocation for high-performing categories such as Fruits & Vegetables.
* Expand operations in Tier 3 cities where demand is growing rapidly.
* Replicate successful practices from medium-sized outlets across other outlet formats.
* Improve low-performing categories through targeted promotions and discounts.
* Continue monitoring customer ratings to maintain high satisfaction levels.

## 🚀 Conclusion

This project demonstrates how business intelligence and data visualization can transform raw sales data into actionable insights. By analyzing sales performance, customer behavior, and outlet efficiency, businesses can make informed decisions that improve profitability, customer satisfaction, and operational effectiveness.
